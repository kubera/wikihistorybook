Students from the University of Applied Sciences Northwestern Switzerland developed a prototype to visualize the idea of pointing out important people from the English Wikipedia by Dr. Peter A. Gloor from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, MIT Center for Collective Intelligence.

Find out more about the idea of the Wikihistorybook at the swarmcreativity blogspot.

Get the code at GitHub.
